Cyb3rhq Cloud service
===================
  
Cyb3rhq is a free and open source platform that offers unified Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) and Extended Detection and Response (XDR) capabilities. It protects workloads across on-premises, virtualized, cloud, and containerized environments.  The Cyb3rhq platform can be deployed in your on-premises and cloud environment or by using our Cyb3rhq Cloud service. 

The Cyb3rhq Cloud service hosts and manages all the Cyb3rhq components in one integrated platform. You can create and tailor your cloud environment to meet your specific needs. The Cyb3rhq Cloud service is streamlined for quick and easy deployment of Cyb3rhq. It handles installation, scaling, and updates of the Cyb3rhq components.

We provide a free trial to create your cloud environment and explore the Cyb3rhq Cloud service.
